Windsong Lyre Keyboard Notes

The Windsong Lyre has several notes:

  • You're limited to C major scale.
  • You can do chords, assuming your keyboard supports 3-key rollover or more.
  • There's also 3 octave limit.

The keyboard mapping is as follows:

hi-octave: q w e r t y u
md-octave: a s d f g h j
lo-octave: z x c v b n m
